Fiction Becomes Reality

Project

Fiction2Reality (F2R) is a long-term, multi-stage project that eventually has us creating the physical buildings of places that are currently fictional (not real). We're starting with Hogwarts and the Backrooms (+ general liminal spaces), but plan to extend beyond just those two fictions.


How It Began

Fiction2Reality began with "What if the Hogwarts castle could exist as a real, explorable place (not just a ride, a set tour, or a few decorated rooms, but the entire building)?" That idea grew into looking into creating a 3D model as a proof of concept. Later, the Backrooms came up as another world we could bring to life. Combining those ideas gave birth to Fiction2Reality: one overall project dedicated to turning fictional worlds into reality, step by step.


The Stages

  1. Digital creations: Proofs of concepts through 3D renders, animations, videos, and other original media.
  2. Business development: Planning, cost analysis, licensing, and partnerships.
  3. Physical builds: Real-world recreations of fictional spaces, beginning with Backrooms-inspired environments, and expanding to Hogwarts and beyond.
